Clocked In — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Clocked In is a simple clock-in and timesheet app for hourly workers, tradespeople, and freelancers. This policy describes what data the app handles and how.

TL;DR

Clocked In is a local-first app. Your time entries, tasks, and notes are stored only on your device. We do not run servers, we do not have user accounts, and we do not collect analytics. The only time data leaves your device is if you choose to send it — by exporting a PDF or image, or sharing through your phone's text message, email, or share sheet.

What data the app stores on your device

  • Time entries: clock-in time, clock-out time, duration, and the task description you entered.
  • App preferences: theme behavior and default hourly wage.

All of the above is stored in your device's local storage (on iOS and Android, this is the app sandbox). Uninstalling the app removes all of this data.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No user accounts — we don't know who you are.
  • No telemetry, crash reporting, or analytics — we don't track how you use the app.
  • No advertising identifiers, no ad tracking, no third-party ad SDKs.
  • No location tracking — Clocked In does not request or use your location.
  • No contacts, photos, or device file access beyond what the system share sheet requires when you tap Export.

Subscription and payment processing

Clocked In Pro is a paid subscription delivered through the Apple App Store (on iOS) and Google Play (on Android). When you subscribe:

  • Apple or Google processes your payment per their own terms — we never see your payment details. Apple's and Google's privacy policies govern that transaction.
  • RevenueCat is our subscription infrastructure provider. The app sends RevenueCat an anonymous user identifier and your subscription status (active / expired / plan tier) so we can unlock paid features. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, time entries, or any other personal data. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.
  • If you cancel, you keep access until the end of the current billing period; after that, paid features lock until you re-subscribe. Your local time entries are never deleted.

Exporting and sharing

When you export a PDF or JPEG, or send your timesheet via text or email, that action uses your device's system share sheet, messages app, or mail client. The data goes wherever you send it — we don't see it and we don't keep a copy.

Children

Clocked In is a professional tool intended for adults tracking their work hours. It is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll change the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be announced in a release note.
